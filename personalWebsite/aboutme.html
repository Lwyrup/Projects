Ryan Clark

Musician @ Solid State Records

About

Ryan Clark (born June 23, 1979) is an American graphic designer and musician who also has performed under the stage name Maven.

He is best known as the lead vocalist of the heavy metal band Demon Hunter, which he co-founded with his brother, guitarist Don Clark. He is featured on Zao's The Lesser Lights of Heaven DVD and Mark Salomon's Podcast, Never Was.
